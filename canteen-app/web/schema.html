CanteenOS Architecture

A comprehensive visual guide to the database schema powering the hostel management, meal planning, and student tracking ecosystem.

Core Entities

The master tables form the foundation of CanteenOS. They store the primary entities: the physical hostels, the students living there, the staff managing them, and the types of meals served.

hostels

MASTER

Physical dormitory buildings that group students and have localized settings.

  • id uuid [PK]
  • name varchar(100)
  • location varchar(200)
  • total_students integer

students

MASTER

Users of the app who book and consume meals. Linked securely via Firebase Auth UID.

  • id uuid [PK]
  • firebase_uid varchar(100) [U]
  • name, phone, email varchar
  • hostel_id uuid [FK]
  • plan varchar(50)

Transaction Records

Highly dynamic tables storing every state change. When a student interacts with the system—booking a meal, scanning a QR code, or leaving feedback—it creates a transaction.

bookings

TRANSACTION

The most critical table. Links a student to a meal type on a specific date, stores their selected items as JSON, and generates a unique QR token.

id uuid [PK]
student_id, hostel_id uuid [FK]
meal_type, booking_date varchar, date
items jsonb → e.g. ["Poha", "Tea"]
order_id varchar(20) [U]
qr_token text

skips

Records when a student opts out of a meal, helping the kitchen reduce food waste.

  • student_id uuid
  • skip_date date
  • reason varchar

qr_scans

Logs exactly when and by whom a booking's QR code was validated at the counter.

  • booking_id uuid
  • scanned_by uuid [staff]
  • is_valid boolean

payments

Tracks financial transactions, such as subscription renewals or guest meal passes.

  • student_id uuid
  • amount decimal
  • transaction_id varchar

Configuration & Logs

System administration tables defining operating hours, tracking auditing, capturing errors, and sending push notifications.

hostel_settings

CONFIG

Stores operating hours for meals and logic constraints like "booking_close_before". Each hostel configures its own rules.

breakfast_start / end time
booking_close_before integer (mins)
max_students_per_slot integer

audit_logs & error_logs

Tracks every data mutation (old/new JSON values) and captures API/System crashes for debugging.

notifications & queue

Generates and queues FCM push notifications (e.g. "Tomorrow's lunch closes in 2 hours!").

Data & Analytics

Aggregated statistical tables for fast dashboard rendering and historical archives to keep active tables light.

daily_stats / monthly_summary

Stores pre-computed sums of bookings, skips, and feedback. Powers the Admin Dashboard's instant load times.

bookings_archive / skips_archive

Historical records moved out of the active transaction tables via automated Cron Jobs at the end of every semester.
